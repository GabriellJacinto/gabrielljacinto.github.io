```elite
Mirror reflex
Void pupil
Sanpaku Full
Where the actual fuck am I again? 
Who the ACTUAL FUCK am I again?
23 revolutions and I'm still dizzy
23 revolutions and I'm still dizzy
23 REVOLUTIONS AND I'M STILL DIZZY
Again
Windless storm
Mute stir
```
\- [[Gabriel Lima Jacinto\|Gabriel Lima Jacinto]], 23 REVOLUTIONS!
